Avocado's: An American Article

With the surge of interest in the green fruit thanks to Millennials and their trendy obsessions, the USA has demonstrated to be one of the countries that best showcases this phenomenon. And these visualisations will help to make their case.

The popularity of Avocado's in America is indisputed. The United States is the second largest producer of avocados after Mexico



The best months to plant your avocado trees are from March through June. However, avocados only thrive in a handful of states.



The name alligator pear originated from a specimen that has a rough skin similar to the hide of Everglades beast of the swamps.



California produces 90% of the U.S. avocado crop. The avocado star of California is San Diego County, which produces 40% of all California-grown avocados.



It is possible for an avocado tree to produce 200 to 300 fruit per tree once it is about 5-7 years of age. The avocado tree, however, alternates bearing. This means that the tree may produce a large crop one year, and then produce a small crop the following year.
